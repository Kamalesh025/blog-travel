Thanjavur Brihadisvara Temple

It is also known as Periya Kovil, RajaRajeswara Temple and Rajarajesvaram.
It is one of the largest temples in India and is an example of Dravidian
architecture during the Chola period. Built by emperor Raja Raja Chola I
and completed in 1010 AD, the temple turned 1000 years old in 2010.

Yercaud

Yercaud is a quaint hill town in South India which is known for its
production of spices, coffee, silk and other textiles. It also has several
attractions including the Loop Road, Yercaud Lake and Kiliyur Waterfalls
that attracts a number of tourists throughout the year.
